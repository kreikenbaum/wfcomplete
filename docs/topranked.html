Does Alexa have a list of its top-ranked websites?

Yes, Alexa publishes the Top 500 Web sites at http://www.alexa.com/topsites.

You can download a free list of the top one million websites ranked by Alexa here. Note that this list is based on the 1 month average Traffic Rank, not the 3-month average traffic rank.  The list is updated daily.

More extensive lists of top sites by country or globally are available programmatically through the Alexa Top Sites Service at http://aws.amazon.com/alexatopsites/.
The number of sites available from the web service depends on how much data we have in each country.  For example,  the lists contain the top 1,000,000 or so sites in the U.S., the top 15,000 or so sites in Brazil and the top 10,000 sites in Sweden.
